# Commodities for Space-Western Trading Game

## Commodity List

1. **Water** - Essential for survival, low value but high demand on dry worlds
2. **Grain** - Staple food crop, basic nutrition source
3. **Meat** - Protein source, perishable, higher value than grain
4. **Spices** - Flavoring and preservation, luxury food additive
5. **Medicine** - Medical supplies, high value, essential for health
6. **Firearms** - Weapons for protection/offense, high value on dangerous worlds
7. **Ammunition** - Weapon accessories, consumed regularly, moderate value
8. **Metals** - Raw materials for construction and manufacturing
9. **Electronics** - High-tech components, essential for advanced civilizations
10. **Narcotics** - Illegal substances, high value but risky to trade

## Supply/Demand Patterns by Planet Type

### Agricultural Planet
- **Supplies**: Water, Grain, Meat, Spices
- **Demands**: Medicine, Firearms, Ammunition, Electronics
- **Ignores**: Metals, Narcotics

### Mega City Planet
- **Supplies**: Electronics, Medicine, Narcotics
- **Demands**: Water, Grain, Meat, Spices, Firearms, Ammunition
- **Ignores**: Metals

### Mining Planet
- **Supplies**: Metals, Electronics
- **Demands**: Water, Food (Grain, Meat), Medicine, Ammunition
- **Ignores**: Spices, Narcotics

### Pirate Space Station
- **Supplies**: Narcotics, Ammunition
- **Demands**: Food (Grain, Meat), Firearms, Medicine
- **Ignores**: Water, Spices, Metals, Electronics

### Research Outpost
- **Supplies**: Electronics, Medicine
- **Demands**: Water, Food (Grain, Meat), Spices
- **Ignores**: Firearms, Ammunition, Metals, Narcotics

### Industrial Planet
- **Supplies**: Electronics, Metals, Ammunition
- **Demands**: Water, Food (Grain, Meat), Medicine
- **Ignores**: Spices, Narcotics

### Frontier Colony
- **Supplies**: Nothing (usually importing everything)
- **Demands**: Everything, but especially Water, Food (Grain, Meat), Medicine, Firearms
- **Ignores**: Narcotics
